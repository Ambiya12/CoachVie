# Design System: CoachVie (Inspired by Tony Robbins Reference)
**Project ID:** Tony-Robbins-Reference

## 1. Atmosphere & Vibe
The design language is **Minimalist Spiritual, High-Energy, and Transformational**. It uses stark contrasts, massive typography, and immersive layout to create a sense of scale and motivation, but strips away visually aggressive elements (gradients, noise) in favor of clear, highly breathable layouts. The pacing of the page strictly alternates between edge-to-edge Absolute White sections and Deep Space Black sections to maintain momentum and rhythm.

## 2. Color Palette
The colors rely entirely on absolute contrast with one core spiritual accent:
- **Absolute White (#FFFFFF):** The breathable canvas for primary content sections.
- **Deep Space Black (#0C0C0C):** Used for stark contrast backgrounds, immersive sections, and heavy typography.
- **Muted Sandstone/Gold (#C2A878):** A calming, premium accent color replacing aggressive tones. Used for subtle highlights and key indicators without being overwhelmingly "charged."
- **Charcoal/Dark Slate (#555555):** Used for secondary text on light backgrounds to maintain hierarchy.

## 3. Typography & Scale
- **Headings:** Monumental, extremely bold (700 wt), sans-serif typography. Heads act as the primary visual anchors. The tracking is extremely tight (`negative letter-spacing`), creating a dense, immediate, and authoritative feel.
- **Body Text:** Clean, legible sans-serif with comfortable line height, playing a highly secondary role to the massive headings.
- **Hierarchy:** High contrast between massive `h1`/`h2` elements and body text. 

## 4. Geometry & Shape
- **Buttons (Pill-shaped):** All primary actions ("Sélectionner cette offre") use fully rounded, pill-shaped buttons (`radius: 9999px`) that contrast starkly with their backgrounds (e.g., white buttons on dark backgrounds, black buttons on light backgrounds).
- **Cards & Containers (Minimal Rounding):** Layout elements and major sections utilize medium rounded corners (`1rem`/`16px`) avoiding any drop shadows, relying entirely on borders and color contrast.
- **Sections (Sharp edges):** Full-width layout blocks strictly use sharp horizontal edges, establishing definitive dark/light boundaries as the user scrolls.

## 5. Depth, Elevation & Photography
- **Flat UI:** The entire interface is completely flat with zero reliance on drop shadows or overlapping elevation. Depth is achieved via pure color contrast and structural sizing.
