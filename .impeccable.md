## Design Context

### Users
General adults improving wellness through structured coaching.

### Brand Personality
Direct, sincere, real, calm, premium.

### Aesthetic Direction
Monochrome black and white UI with balanced impact.
Inter remains the font.
Photos and videos remain full color.
Section order remains unchanged.

### Design Principles
1. Keep the primary call to action clear and immediate.
2. Use compact spacing rhythm; avoid macro empty space.
3. Avoid oversized components; keep proportionate scale.
4. Preserve readability and accessibility across breakpoints.
5. Use subtle, purposeful motion with reduced-motion support.
